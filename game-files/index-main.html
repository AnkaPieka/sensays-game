For years, you've train for this moment... Finally, it's time ! Time to show your talent to the legendary and mighty Ishikawa sensei.

He's well known to be the best teacher in all Okinawa... and the grumpiest one, too. With him, no second chance.

You'll have to earn his respect reproducing his moves perfectly. If you're lucky - and talented - maybe he'll accept you as a student.

Each technique is linked to a keyboard arrow. Remember them all...

This move is called shuto. It's an open hand technique, meant to be like a blade.

This move is called oi zuki. It's a stepping punch.

Mawashi geri is actually quite famous : it's a round kick.

Finally, Mae geri, is a front kick.

Once you'll be inside Ishikawa sensei's dojo, you'll be able to indicate your level : white belt, brown belt or black belt. Choose wisely as you won't be able to change your level until you complete the serie... or loose.

Ready ? Hajime !
